"Driving Africa’s Sustainable Future through Innovation and Global Collaboration"

About Us

JPH Group is a pioneering African technology consultancy focused on delivering eco-friendly, innovative solutions to support sustainable growth across the continent. As a product developer, manufacturer, and distributor, JPH Group offers high-tech advancements tailored to the unique needs of African industries.

Our Social Impact

At JPH Group, we believe in technology’s transformative potential to improve lives and communities. Every initiative we undertake is rooted in a commitment to uplift African industries while minimizing environmental impact.

Global Network and Partnerships

Our network spans continents, partnering with like-minded organizations dedicated to shaping a sustainable future. By collaborating with global innovators, non-profit groups, and sustainable development leaders, JPH Group ensures that our solutions are both cutting-edge and aligned with global best practices.

Funding and Project Safety

With JPH Group, funders and investors gain the confidence that their contributions are stewarded responsibly and effectively. We create a secure, unified framework to streamline the ideation, planning, and delivery of impactful projects.

Global Magnitude Events

JPH Group has a track record of supporting high-impact global events, where we contribute at every stage—from conception and strategic planning to on-the-ground delivery.

Leadership

Under the visionary leadership of CEO/Founder Janyne Almheiri, COO/Partner Rene Tammemäe, and Business Development Executive/Partner Stephen Bitok, JPH Group continues to expand its influence as a trailblazer in African technology and sustainability.

Vision

To be a global leader in sustainable technology solutions, empowering Africa’s industries to thrive through innovation and environmental stewardship..

Mission

To deliver cutting-edge, eco-friendly products and services that support Africa’s industrial growth, foster sustainability, and create lasting social and economic impact.

Objectives

  • Sustainable Growth: Promote eco-friendly solutions that drive economic development while conserving resources.
  • Community Impact: Improve lives by prioritizing initiatives that benefit society and the environment
  • Empowering Industries: Equip African businesses with advanced technologies tailored to regional challenges and opportunities.
  • Global Partnerships: Forge alliances with international organizations to bring world-class standards to Africa’s development projects.
  • Innovation Leadership: Continuously innovate to remain at the forefront of sustainable technologies.

Core Values

  • Innovation: Pursue excellence through creativity and cutting-edge technology.
  • Integrity: Operate with transparency, accountability, and trustworthiness
  • Collaboration: Build strong partnerships to achieve shared goals
  • Community-Centered: Focus on creating solutions that uplift and benefit local communities
  • Sustainability: Commitment to environmental responsibility in all our operations.
